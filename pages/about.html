Our Mission

At Rejoice Institute House, we believe in the transformative power of storytelling. Our mission is to cultivate, publish, and celebrate works that challenge perspectives, inspire change, and connect humanity through the written word.

We are committed to discovering emerging voices while honoring established literary traditions, creating a bridge between generations of readers and writers.

Our Journey

Foundation

Rejoice Institute House was founded with the vision of creating a literary sanctuary for innovative voices, a place of access for creatives to see, experience, and successfully publish their creative works.

2024

"Zoe" & "The Challenge" became our first publications, establishing our reputation for quality storytelling and reader engagement.
