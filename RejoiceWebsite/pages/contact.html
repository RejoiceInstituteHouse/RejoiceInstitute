Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my manuscript?

We accept manuscript submissions through our online portal. Please use the contact form above and select "Manuscript Submission" as your subject, and we'll send you our submission guidelines.

Do you accept international authors?

Absolutely! We welcome submissions from authors worldwide. We have a dedicated team that handles international publications and translations.

Can I schedule a meeting with an editor?

Yes, we offer consultation meetings. Please email our editorial department to schedule an appointment. Include a brief overview of your project in your request.

Do you host book launch events?

We regularly host book launches, readings, and author signings. Contact our events team for more information about hosting your event with us.
